# 🧟 Resident Evil 2 — Full Achievement Guide

An interactive, single-page web guide for **Resident Evil 2 Remake (2019)** — built for tracking all 44 achievements, with Steam sync, puzzle codes, boss tips, and collectible locations.

**Live site:** [emmaoudhof.github.io/Resident-Evil-2-Full-Guide](**https://emmaoudhof.github.io/Resident-Evil-2-Full-Guide/)

---

## Features

- **Steam Sync** — Enter your Steam vanity URL or 64-bit Steam ID to automatically import your unlocked achievements
- **44 Achievements** — All achievements listed with descriptions, tags (Story / Missable / Leon / Claire / Challenge / Ghost Survivors), and filterable views
- **Progress Bar** — Live completion counter (X/44) that updates as you sync or manually check off achievements
- **Safes & Locks** — All combinations, locker codes, safe dials, medallion solutions, and lab puzzle answers for both Scenario A and B
- **Mr. Raccoon Locations** — All 15 figurine locations with descriptions
- **Boss Fights** — Weak points and strategy tips for every boss encounter
- **Files & Notes** — All 58 collectible files listed with locations, required for the *Lore Explorer* achievement
- **Key Items** — All four RPD keys with locations and how to obtain them
- **Film Rolls** — All 3 hint film rolls with full step-by-step solutions and rewards

---

## Steam Sync

1. Make sure your Steam profile **Game Details** are set to **Public**
   - Steam → Profile → Edit Profile → Privacy Settings → Game Details → Public
2. Enter your **vanity URL** (e.g. `belleke` from `steamcommunity.com/id/belleke`) or your **64-bit Steam ID** (find it at [steamid.io](https://steamid.io))
3. Click **Sync Achievements** — your unlocked achievements will be checked off automatically
4. Click **Disconnect** to clear everything and start fresh

> Progress is **not** saved between sessions — sync with Steam each visit to see your current state.
